Getting Started
This event will have exclusive rules that promote cards from the archetypes in the Solo Mode scenarios, such as Monarch, Elementsaber, and World Chalice. There are some cards that cannot be used as they are not part of an archetype, or belong to an archetype that has not been featured in Solo Mode as of January 2023.
Since this is a massive banlist, in lieu of posting all of the banned cards, we're going to give examples of popular cards that are banned. There are several non-Story Mode archetypes that have had several key cards hit, so if you want to check it out for yourself, check out the event banlist in-game by entering the Event Mode in the Deck Menu!
Tier 1 : The most successful Tournament Topping Decks, with power levels of at least 12.
Due to the latest set release, the metagame is likely to change rapidly. The decks shown here may be much weaker in upcoming tournaments, and other decks may be much stronger. Check back in a couple weeks to see how the new format has developed!
